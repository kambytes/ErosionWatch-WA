New lens on coastal change

ErosionWatch-WA integrates geospatial datasets and models to monitor and forecast erosion along WA's coastline.

▾

What is ErosionWatch-WA?

ErosionWatch-WA is an interactive web application developed to address the eroding coastlines of Western Australia. It provides an interactive interface for users to visualise the historical shoreline changes and predict future erosion patterns along Western Australia's coastlines. By utilising publicly available datasets from DataWA, satellite imagery and AI forecasting, this tool delivers insights into our changing coastlines.
